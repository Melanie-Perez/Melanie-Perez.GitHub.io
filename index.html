Good afternoon, I am a 2nd year student at the University of California, Riverside. I am currently majoring in Political Science - Public Service. Once, I helped get basic information from the people getting food who only spoke Spanish.

At the Smart & Final, I mostly spent my time cleaning, facing store items, or restocking store items. While I did that, I would also help direct customers to where an item could be found. This included providing translation for Spanish speakers. Additionally, I would go on cart runs, where I would push the carts from the parking lot back to the store entrance.
